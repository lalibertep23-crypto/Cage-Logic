# Cage Logic — Demo Walkthrough Script (two-fold)

**For:** Chris · **Audience:** Frankie (then Ricardo) · **Tool:** `CageLogic_Demo_Prototype.html` on your phone.
**Structure:** lead with retention (Frankie's pain) → pivot to the upside (UFC pipeline). Let the app do the talking. Hand him the phone.

---

## Open — name the problem

> "Frank, you asked me to fix retention without building another gym app. I didn't build a gym app. I built the thing that keeps your white belts from quitting at week six — and a few other things. Let me show you."

Open the prototype to the menu. Hand him the phone.

---

# PART ONE — YOUR GYM (retention)

## 1. Day One (tap "Day One")

Let him tap through it.

> "This is what a new white belt sees their first day. Your welcome, in your words. We make them write down *why* they walked in — and we show it back to them at the exact moment they want to quit."

When he hits the **wall screen**:
> "We tell them the wall is coming — weeks 5 to 8 — before they hit it. Most gyms pretend it's not there. That honesty is what gets them through it. That's retention."

## 2. Coach view (back to menu → tap "Coach")

> "This is your side. Every member, and the ones drifting — here's who hasn't shown in 9, 12, 15 days, and *why they joined* right next to their name. You reach out before they're gone, while it still means something. And it flags who's due for a stripe."

## 3. The math (hand him the one-pager — `Retention_Case`)

> "Here's what that's worth. At industry retention you're leaking around $160K a year out the door. A 15% lift — modest — is about $42K a year back. Against a few hundred bucks. Plug in your real numbers; it still pays for itself many times over."

*Pause here. This is the buy-in. Let him react before you move on.*

---

# PART TWO — THE UPSIDE (the pipeline)

## 4. The Profile (back to menu → tap "Athlete")

Let him look at his own profile.

> "Now the upside. This is your profile. Record, fight reel, black belt — verified under Ricardo. Ten seconds and a matchmaker knows exactly who they're looking at. Every athlete on here gets one. That's the scouting layer UFC BJJ doesn't have."

## 5. Comp Prep + Session Log (tap COMP, then LOG)

> "UFC BJJ Opens, August 22, counting down — the app builds the taper from the date. And underneath it all, the session log: every roll, every technique, what to ask the coach. Nobody's built this for grappling."

---

## The ask

1. **"Call Ricardo."** Would he make the intro to UFC BJJ.
2. **"Get five of your guys registered for August 22."** Their profiles are the proof.
3. **"Put your son on the wrestling module this season."** First wrestling pilot.

> "One call to Ricardo. Five athletes in Vegas. Your son on wrestling. The rest builds from there."

---

## Reminders

- **Lead with retention.** That's what Frankie cares about. The UFC stuff is the upside, not the opener.
- It's a prototype — if a tap doesn't work, "that's the next build."
- Profile = his real record. Coach/Comp/Log = sample layout. Say so if asked.
